About

I build practical cybersecurity research artifacts: CTI field manuals, actor-focused knowledge bases, detection workflows, malware-analysis tools, OpenCTI enrichment projects, cloud-security labs, and long-form technical guides.

  • Research should become reusable outputs, not only articles.
  • CTI output should show evidence, gaps, assumptions, confidence, and operational use.
  • Outputs include hunting hypotheses, detection backlog items, YARA, infrastructure pivots, SOC handoff notes, and prioritized remediation logic.
  • Offensive-informed labs are used as controlled context for defensive reasoning.
