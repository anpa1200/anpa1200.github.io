# CTI Analyst Field Manual

The CTI Analyst Field Manual is a public methodology resource for cyber threat intelligence analysis, evidence handling, ATT&CK mapping, and CTI-to-detection workflows.

## Intended Use

- Structure CTI collection and review
- Separate evidence from inference
- Map behaviors to ATT&CK techniques
- Identify required telemetry
- Convert intelligence into detection candidates
- Track validation gaps and analyst decisions
