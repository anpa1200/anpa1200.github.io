# Security policy

## Reporting a vulnerability

Email `1200km@gmail.com` with the affected URL or component, impact, safe
reproduction steps, and any supporting evidence. Do not include secrets,
credentials, personal data, or customer data in the first message.

Reports are acknowledged when possible, triaged according to impact and
reproducibility, and coordinated before public disclosure. No bounty, response
time, or remediation deadline is promised.

## Scope

This policy covers the public static content and deployment code for
`https://1200km.com/` and repositories explicitly owned by `anpa1200`.
Third-party services, external publications, local-only lab systems, and other
people's infrastructure are out of scope.

This policy does not grant permission for denial of service, destructive
testing, social engineering, credential attacks, persistence, data access,
privacy violations, or testing against systems you do not own or have explicit
authorization to assess. Use only minimal, non-destructive validation.

For the self-hosted AdversaryGraph application/API, coordinate a controlled
test deployment before testing authenticated or state-changing behavior.
